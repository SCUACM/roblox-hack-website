About
What is a game jam?

A game jam is a collaborative event where you and a team create a video game from scratch based on a theme within 24 hours.

What is Roblox?

Roblox is a leading online video game company for users to design, create, and play games. Roblox provides a platform for aspiring game developers to showcase their creativity and for players to explore a wide array of games within a single ecosystem.

Who can attend?

Roblox Hack is open to all Santa Clara University students and majors. We strongly encourage and welcome everyone from any background and experience level to come. This is the perfect opportunity for all skill levels to learn more about the world of game development!




Feel free to email us at acm@scu.edu if you have any questions or concerns!
