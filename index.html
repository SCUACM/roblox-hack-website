About
What is Roblox Week?

Roblox Week is a week-long experience sponsored by Roblox where you and a team of up to 6 will learn, be mentored, and ultimately create a video game in Roblox from scratch based on a theme. Roblox Week will be a mix between workshops taught by Roblox employees and a 48 hour game jam, all virtual.

What is Roblox?

Roblox is a leading online video game company for users to design, create, and play games. Roblox provides a platform for aspiring game developers to showcase their creativity and for players to explore a wide array of games within a single ecosystem.

Who can attend?

Roblox Week is open to all Santa Clara University students and majors. We strongly encourage and welcome everyone from any background and experience level to come. This is the perfect opportunity for all skill levels to learn more about the world of game development!
